French Toast

French toast is a delicious breakfast dish made by dipping slices of bread in a mixture of beaten eggs, milk, and often cinnamon and vanilla, then frying them until golden brown. It's typically served with toppings like syrup, powdered sugar, fresh fruit, or whipped cream. This recipe is enough to serve 2 people.

Ingredients
Quantity Ingredient Instruction
4 slices Bread (preferably day-old) Slightly stale
2 large Eggs Beaten
1/2 cup Milk Whole or 2%
1 tsp Cinnamon Ground
1 tsp Vanilla Extract Optional
For frying Butter or Oil As needed
For serving Maple Syrup, Powdered Sugar, Fresh Fruit, etc. As desired

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs, milk, cinnamon, and vanilla extract in a shallow dish.
  2. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, coating both sides.
  3. Heat butter or oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Fry the bread slices until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve with maple syrup, powdered sugar, whipped cream, butter, or fresh fruit as desired.
